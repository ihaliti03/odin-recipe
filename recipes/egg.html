Egg recipe

Description

Now you might think that this is some BS. Why would anyone give egg recipe?!

However, little did you know. Eggs are very high in protein and very crucial to grow muscles (13g of protein per egg, jim rats love it).

Ingredients

  • Eggs of course
  • Oil
  • Vegetta

Steps

  1. Put the pan on the fire
  2. Put some oil and wait for it to heat
  3. Crack the eggs and put them on the pan
  4. Little bit of vegetta from the top makes it tasty
  5. Wait for it to cook and to look nice
