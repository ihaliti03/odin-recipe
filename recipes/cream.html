Cake's Cream

Description

Here we are gonna show a recipe of the cream (creme) that the inside of cake is filled with, the amazing part is the delicious taste of the cream and it`s high quality.

The prize of the cake is the cream and that's what makes the cake itself so tasty.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Whipped cream (Liquid state)
  • Water
  • Cream flour

Steps

  1. 2l water mix with 2l of whipped cream
  2. Mix 2l of milk with that too until it comes to a solid state
  3. Mix it all now with the flour
